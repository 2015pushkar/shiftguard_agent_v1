# Timekeeping Policy

## Clock-In and Clock-Out
Employees must record a clock-in punch at the start of every shift and a
clock-out punch at the end. Worked time for a shift is the elapsed time between
the clock-in and clock-out punches, less any unpaid meal break.

## Missed Clock-Out
A shift that has a clock-in punch but no clock-out punch is incomplete. Payroll
must not estimate or auto-fill the missing punch. The shift is flagged for
manager correction, and any hours that depend on the missing punch are treated
as unverified until corrected.

## Meal Breaks
Shifts longer than 6 hours include a mandatory 30-minute unpaid meal break. The
unpaid meal break is deducted from worked time for that shift. Breaks shorter
than 20 minutes are paid and are not deducted.

## Workweek Definition
For timekeeping and overtime, the workweek runs Monday 12:00 AM through Sunday
11:59 PM. Each shift is attributed to the workweek that contains its clock-in
punch.
